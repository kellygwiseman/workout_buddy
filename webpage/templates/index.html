Workout Buddy
A personal trainer at your fingertips


Workout Buddy counts exercise repetitions and assesses move quality to provide real-time feedback and tracking capabilities to fitness lovers. The beta version starts out tracking pushups, with more moves to come!



All you need is your iPhone and an armaband to get started analyzing your fitness cabalities. Workout Buddy uses your phone's accelerometer and gyro sensor, along with an ensemble of signal processing filters and classification models, to rate your workout.
